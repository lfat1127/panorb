PanOrb Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to setup questions, media requirements, and common troubleshooting steps.

What is PanOrb? +

PanOrb is a software application designed to provide users with a comprehensive platform for managing digital media content.

How do I install PanOrb? +

Desktop

Visit panorb.desolatestudio.dev. Desktop currently supports images, and video. Support for online media is planned.

Mobile Apps

Download PanOrb from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store by searching for "PanOrb" and following your device's installation steps.

Mobile supports both images and videos on device. For online media, image support is available now and video support is planned.

Is PanOrb free to use? +

Yes, PanOrb is free to use.

Bugs and issues +
  1. Ensure your images or videos use a 2:1 ratio. If media does not match this ratio, it may not display correctly.
  2. If media is not displaying, refresh the page or restart the application. If the issue persists, contact support.
  3. On mobile, try clearing app cache or reinstalling the app if you encounter issues.
  4. On some Android phones, video playback may appear laggy or not smooth due to limited processor performance, high video resolution, or high bitrates. For a better playback experience, try using a more powerful phone or reduce the video's resolution and bitrate.
Why is my media not found in the mobile list? +

Make sure PanOrb has permission to access photos/videos in your device settings.

Also verify that media files follow the required 2:1 ratio so they can appear correctly in the list.
