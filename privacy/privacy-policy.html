Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how PanOrb ("the App") handles information when you use the app on iOS and Android.

Last updated: June 20, 2026

1. Information We Process

Category What is processed Why Where it is handled
Photo Library access Photos and videos from your device library that the app can access after you grant media permission. To display 360 media in the gallery and open selected items in the viewer. On-device via Apple Photos / iOS Photos permissions and Android media permissions.
Photo save permission The image you explicitly choose to save from the viewer. To add exported images to your photo library. On-device via iOS Photos APIs and Android storage/media APIs.
URL input and network requests URLs you enter manually, plus related HTTP responses needed to load image content. To validate and download images from links you provide. Sent to the host you specify in the URL. The app does not operate its own upload server for this feature.
Local app preferences Language preference and recent links list. To remember your settings and improve convenience. Stored locally on your device (UserDefaults on iOS, SharedPreferences on Android).
Advertising data (Google AdMob) Ad request, impression, click, and device/app context data processed by Google Mobile Ads SDK. To display in-app banner ads and measure ad performance. Processed by Google according to its own policies.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) for banner ads.
  • Firebase Core is initialized for Google service configuration in the app build.

According to the app configuration, Firebase Analytics is set to disabled in the bundled configuration file. Third-party providers may still process technical data required to deliver their services.

See also: Google Privacy Policy

3. Sharing and Disclosure

  • The app does not require account registration and does not sell your personal data.
  • Content may be shared only when you actively use iOS or Android sharing actions or interact with third-party ad services.
  • URLs you enter are transmitted to the destination host to retrieve content; this is necessary for URL loading.

4. Data Retention and Control

  • Recent links and language preference remain on-device until you clear app data or uninstall the app.
  • You can clear recent links inside the app.
  • You can manage media/photo permissions anytime in iOS or Android system settings.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided data through the app, contact us so we can review and address the issue.

6. Security

We use platform-provided iOS and Android APIs and reasonable technical measures to handle data, but no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date on this page.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@desolatestudio.dev
